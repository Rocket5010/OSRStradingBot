# ma_crossover

**Family:** trend/momentum (good in trending markets). Part of the [[Strategy System]].

## Buy
**Golden cross** — fast MA crosses above slow MA.

## Sell
**Death cross** — fast MA crosses below slow MA. Stop-loss below.

## Params
`fast_ma, slow_ma, stop_loss_pct, max_hold_days`

## Notes
Classic trend-follower. Compared in [[Backtesting]].
